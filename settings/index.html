Home Connect - pair appliances

Use the one-time cloud login to retrieve the local keys for your Bosch/Siemens appliances. After that, the app talks locally and you can block the appliances from the internet. Add discovered appliances via Add device > Home Connect.

The SingleKey ID login cannot run inside the app because the login page is protected by hCaptcha. Log in with your own browser instead.

  1. Generate the login link and open it in your browser, or copy it.
  2. Log in with SingleKey ID. Your browser will try to open hcauth://auth/prod?code=... and show an error. That is expected.
  3. Copy the full hcauth:// URL from the address bar or network inspector and paste it below.
